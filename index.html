粒子如何诞生How particles emerge

WEI 通过一条双曲凝聚曲线（Hyperbolic Coalescence Curve）在 Uniswap V4 Hook 上诞生。WEI is born on a Hyperbolic Coalescence Curve implemented as a Uniswap V4 Hook.

用户只有两个动作：Users have only two actions:

Mint — 存入 ETH → 释放粒子Mint — deposit ETH → release particles Burn — 销毁 WEI → 返还 ETHBurn — destroy WEI → return ETH

储备池是 WEI 的本体。每一枚流通的 WEI，背后都对应着真实、不可提取的 ETH。The reserve pool is the substance of WEI. Every circulating WEI maps to real, non‑extractable ETH.

凝聚曲线Condensation curve

已释放 WEI 数量（当前 ETH 储备为 R）WEI released when ETH reserve is R

\[ M(R) = 10^{9} \cdot \frac{R}{R + 155.9} \]

当前铸造价格（每枚 WEI，关于储备 R）Current mint price per WEI as a function of R

\[ p(R) = \frac{155.9}{10^{9}} \left( 1 + \frac{R}{155.9} \right)^{2} \]

销毁 b 枚 WEI 可获得的 ETHETH received when burning b WEI

\[ \Delta e(R,\, b) = 155.9 \cdot \left( \frac{M(R)}{K - M(R)} - \frac{M(R) - b}{K - M(R) + b} \right) \quad,\quad K = 10^{9} \]

曲线特性：Curve properties:

  • 早期储备低时，粒子容易凝聚（成本低）Early on, low reserves make coalescence easy (low cost)
  • 随着储备增加，凝聚难度加速上升As reserves grow, difficulty rises sharply
  • 当储备达到一定规模后，铸造成本极高，发行自然停止At scale, mint cost becomes prohibitive and issuance stops naturally
  • 理论最大释放量 10 亿枚；实际可达量接近约 9.9 亿枚Theoretical max release 1B tokens; practical ceiling ~990M

黑洞机制Black hole mechanism

每一次 Mint 或 Burn，收取 0.1559% 协议费，100% 直接永久燃烧（打入黑洞），不进入储备。Each Mint or Burn charges a 0.1559% protocol fee; 100% is burned permanently (sent to the black hole), never to reserves.

行为Action 发生什么What happens
Mint 部分 WEI 直接被烧入黑洞（协议费路径）A portion of WEI is burned straight to the black hole (fee path)
Burn 对应比例的 WEI 被直接烧入黑洞（协议费路径）A proportional share of WEI is burned to the black hole (fee path)

这一机制让每一次交互都真实减少 WEI 总供应。10 亿是规则，燃烧是现实：最终流通量将自然低于 10 亿枚。Every interaction reduces WEI supply in practice. One billion is the rule; burning is reality—circulating supply will fall below 1B.
